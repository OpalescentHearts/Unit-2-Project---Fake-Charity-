Winona Ryder:
The Greatest Actress of All Time!


Brief History of her Acting Career.

Winona Ryder is an astonishing Actress known for her unique and quirky roles.
Although her acting career began with her role as "Rina" in the movie Lucas her
career truly began when she was noticed for her role as "Lydia Deetz"
from Beetlejuice.
She'd go on to play many roles over the years ranging from characters such as
Veronica Sawyer from Heathers to "Joyce Byers" from Stranger Things.

What makes her the GOAT?

Her acting career hit a bump and she took a four-year hiatus from 2001 to 2005.
When she came back despite the ups and downs she kept going.
Playing many quirky characters throughout the years.
Her ability to play a range of unique characters is truly what makes
me believe she is the GOAT.
